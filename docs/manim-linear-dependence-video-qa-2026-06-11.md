## Scope

This pass adds a trilingual Manim `VideoExplanation` to MATH1030 `6.4 Linear
dependence and independence`. The video focuses on the source-supported idea
that dependence means redundancy: a vector stops adding a new direction when it
falls inside the span of the previous vectors, which becomes a nontrivial
relation and appears as a free column in the homogeneous-system test.

The video is embedded after the redundancy and row-reduction theorem cards and
before the proof section. The existing `independence-checker` remains the
reader-controlled follow-up.
