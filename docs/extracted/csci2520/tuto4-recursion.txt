# Slide 4
Recursion
Recursion
 is a method where the solution to a problem depends on solutions to 
smaller instances of the same problem
.
Recursion usually leads to more 
elegant
 and 
simpler
 solutions, although it incurs larger memory and time overhead.
An important recursive problem-solving skill is 
divide-and-conquer
.
Divide
 the problem into smaller pieces.
Tackle
 each sub-task either directly or by recursion.
Combine
 the solutions of the parts to form the solution of the whole

# Slide 6
Greatest Common Divisor
The greatest common divisor (GCD) of 
two or more 
integers, when at least one of them is not zero, is the largest positive integer that divides the numbers without a remainder.
For example, the GCD of 8 and 12 is 4.
